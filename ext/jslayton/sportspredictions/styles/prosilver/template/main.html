Terms and Conditions of Entry

Entry is free, however to be eligible for a prize, tippers must have a minimum of 10 posts on the forums by the completion of the AFL and NRL seasons.

Tipping Competition Prizes

Prizes will be given to the participants who meeting the above criteria, finish 1st, 2nd and 3rd in either the AFL or NRL tipping competitions. The three tippers who have the highest total number of correctly selected winning teams in all 23 rounds of the AFL season plus finals or all 26 rounds of the NRL season plus finals for each respective competition, will be eligible for the prizes on offer. The prizes on offer for each competition are as follows:

1st Prize - $200.00 cash + $100.00 Rebel Sport gift voucher
2nd Prize - $100.00 cash + $50.00 Rebel Sport gift voucher
3rd Prize - $50.00 cash + $30.00 Rebel Sport gift voucher

Tipping Competition Rules

Entering tips for each game will close at the time indicated in the upcoming games list. If your tips are not placed before the close of each game, you will receive no points for that game. Please do not ask for your tips to be entered after the set game time listed has passed. If you did not enter your tips then that is bad luck.

The allocation of points is as follows:

1 point for each correct tip
1 bonus point for a correct tip in the match of the round
3 bonus points for correctly tipping an entire round
0 points given for an incorrect tip

1 bonus point for correctly tipping a draw

Determination of Winners

If at the end of the tipping competition two (2) or more tippers have the same amount of points, the tipper with the best tips win percentage will be determined as the higher placed tipper.

Claiming of Prizes

Cash prizes can be claimed once each of the AFL & NRL grand finals are completed. Payment can be processed via either direct deposit into a nominated bank account or money order through Australia Post. Gift vouchers will be sent via post.

Prizes must be claimed within 4 weeks of the respective competition close and all monetary prizes will be paid in Australia Dollars (AUD).
